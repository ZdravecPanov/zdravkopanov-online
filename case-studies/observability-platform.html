Observability Platform — Grafana + Zabbix

Unified monitoring, alerting, and dashboards for infrastructure and service health.

Overview

Built a centralized observability platform using Zabbix for metrics and alerting and Grafana for executive and operations dashboards. The goal was to replace fragmented monitoring with a single source of truth for infrastructure health, incident response, and SLA reporting.

Scope: 50+ hosts, 12 critical services
Timeline: 4 weeks
Stack: Zabbix, Grafana, Linux, Alerting
Role: DevOps Engineer

Challenge

Monitoring was split across tools and teams. Alert fatigue, missing baselines, and inconsistent dashboards made incident response slow and unreliable.

Goals

  • Consolidate metrics and alerts into a single platform.
  • Standardize dashboards for infrastructure, services, and SLA reporting.
  • Reduce alert noise with tuned thresholds and routing rules.
  • Enable on-call teams to identify root cause in minutes.

Architecture

Hosts / Services
      |
  Zabbix Agents
      |
 Zabbix Server
      | \
      |  -> Alerting (Email / Chat / On-call)
      |
Grafana (Zabbix Datasource)
      |
Dashboards + SLA Reports

Dashboards

  • Infrastructure Health: CPU, memory, disk I/O, network saturation.
  • Service Availability: uptime, response time, error rates.
  • Capacity Planning: growth trends, saturation forecasts.
  • SLA Reporting: monthly uptime and incident summaries.

Alerting Strategy

  • Tiered severity levels with routing to the right on-call group.
  • Suppression rules for maintenance windows and known noisy hosts.
  • Actionable alerts that include runbook links and remediation steps.
  • Escalation policy for critical services with automated paging.

Implementation Highlights

  • Standardized Zabbix templates across Linux and network devices.
  • Grafana dashboards built with consistent naming and tags.
  • Integrated alert notifications with Slack and email channels.
  • Documented runbooks aligned to top incident categories.

Results

  • Reduced alert noise by 40% through tuned triggers.
  • Improved incident triage time with unified dashboards.
  • Created a single reporting view for leadership and ops.
