Observability Platform

Unified metrics, logs, and alerting for faster incident response.

Overview

Built a production-ready observability stack that consolidated metrics, logs, and alerts into service-level dashboards. The goal was to reduce alert fatigue and enable faster root-cause analysis.

Scope: 40+ hosts
Timeline: 5 weeks
Stack: Prometheus, Grafana, Loki, Alertmanager
Role: Observability Lead

Challenge

Monitoring was fragmented and produced noisy alerts. Incident response relied on manual log collection and repeated context switching across tools.

Approach

  • Standardized service metrics with consistent labels.
  • Implemented alert routing based on severity and ownership.
  • Created dashboards aligned to SLOs and business services.
  • Defined playbooks to accelerate triage and escalation.

Architecture

Applications -> Prometheus -> Alertmanager -> On-call
      |             |
    Loki --------> Grafana

Results

  • Reduced alert noise by 40% with tuned thresholds.
  • Cut MTTR by 25% through shared dashboards.
  • Improved visibility for on-call and product teams.
